    <h3>List of ingredients</h3>
    
    <h4>Protein:</h4>
    <ul>
    <li>2 boneless and skinless chicken breasts</li>
    <li>1 cup of low-fat cottage cheese</li>
    <li>½ cup of plain non-fat greek yogurt</li>
    <li>¾ of grated parmesan cheese</li>
    <li>8 oz of high protein pasta</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h4>Veggies:</h4>
    <ul>
    <li>2 cups of broccoli florets</li>
    <li>3 cloves of garlic minced</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h4>Sauce:</h4>
    <ul>
    <li>1 cup of low-fat dairy milk (but you can also use unsweetened almond milk)</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon of olive oil or light butter</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon of all-purpose flour</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h4>seasonings:</h4>
    <ul>
    <li>Red pepper flakes (optional but my personal favorite)
</li>
    <li>Garlic powder (also optional)</li>
    <li>Salt and pepper to liking</li>
    </ul>
    
    <img src="https://assets.content.technologyadvice.com/635755897805293546_b4860b3010.webp" alt="red pepper and garlic powder" width="400" height="400">
    
    <h3>Basic cooking instructions (in steps):</h3>
    <ol>
      <li>(0-5 min) Put large pot of water to boil, cut chiken breasts into small pieces, chop
broccoli into florets, and then mince garlic.</li>
      <br>
      <li>(5-15 min) start cooking the pasta, sauté chicken in alive oil/ light butter, then
microwave broccoli.</li>
      <br>
      <li>(15-22 min) Remove chicken from pot and sauté the pot with garlic, add milk and a bit of
flour, stir in cottage cheese and greek yogurt and parmesan, then simmer gently for 3 min.</li>
      <br>
      <li>(22-30 min)  Add chicken and broccoli back to pan, toss in drained pasta, adjust the salt
and pepper and any other seasoning, and you are done!</li>
    </ol>
